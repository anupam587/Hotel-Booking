Genral Information

The Graywood House Hotel is located in a tranquil, residential area of the city which affords guests some relief from the hustle and bustle of the in-town environs. There is public transportation located within walking distance, however, which can take guests to locations throughout the city proper. The airport is located approximately 7 miles from the Graywood Bhawan Heritage House Hotel.
The rooms that the Graywood Bhawan Heritage House Hotel open up on to balconies which overlook either the pool or the gardens. There is cable television service, air-conditioning and a telephone provided in each room. The private baths feature toiletries and hairdryers provided by the hotel.
The Graywood Bhawan Heritage House Hotel is built and decorated in the traditional Rajasthani fashion and features carved balconies, terraces, gardens and courtyards. Within the hotel there is a rooftop restaurant and a fitness center. There is an outdoor swimming pool. The hotel does have onsite security. There is a 24-hour front desk at this hotel and guests may take advantage of the safe deposit boxes.
